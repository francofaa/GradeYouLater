• GradeYouLater is a simple webpage to help you grade. Enter in the assignment and its total score and the student and their total score. Add one to the student's score by using the big plus and minus buttons. Generate a percentage grade and post the grade to a table at the bottom. You can export that table to a spreadsheet.
